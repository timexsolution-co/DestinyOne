# DestinyOne Community Guidelines

DestinyOne is for adults seeking committed, long-term relationships or marriage. Every interaction should protect dignity, consent, authenticity, and safety.

## Be genuine

- Use your real identity, accurate age, current relationship status, and recent photos of yourself.
- Do not impersonate, catfish, misrepresent intentions, or create accounts for another person.
- Do not promote casual encounters, affairs, escort services, recruitment, or commercial arrangements.

## Respect consent and boundaries

- Accept “no,” a declined match, an unmatch, or silence without retaliation.
- Do not pressure anyone to share contact details, photos, location, money, or personal documents.
- Never share private conversations or intimate material without explicit permission.

## Keep the community safe

- No threats, stalking, harassment, hate, bullying, exploitation, trafficking, or violence.
- No sexual content involving minors; minors may not use DestinyOne.
- No scams, investment pitches, crypto requests, gift-card requests, payment solicitation, spam, malware, or account trading.
- Do not ask virtual-gift recipients for anything in return. Gifts do not buy attention, contact, or dates.

## Meet thoughtfully

- Keep early conversations in DestinyOne, meet in public, use your own transportation, and tell a trusted person your plans.
- Do not publish another member’s exact location. Crossed Paths and date ideas should use approximate areas only.
- Report urgent danger to local emergency services; in-app reporting is not an emergency channel.

## Reporting and enforcement

Use Report, Block, or Unmatch whenever needed. Reports are private from the reported member. DestinyOne may remove content, restrict features, suspend accounts, permanently ban members, preserve narrow safety records, and refer serious matters to authorities when legally appropriate.
