# DestinyOne P1 operations plan

P1 is the bridge between a polished MVP and a real city-by-city dating business. Backend credentials can still be connected later, but these operating systems need clear ownership before launch growth.

## P1 areas now tracked in the app

1. **Live venue/date marketplace**
   - Current: marketplace preview, search, safe-place filters, date packages, partner intake, live-ops readiness scoring and city coverage exist.
   - Pending: live venue provider, reservation provider, hours/ratings/maps, payment/refund webhooks, inventory and staffed support SLAs.

2. **City growth backend**
   - Current: NYC, Bay Area, Dallas, Toronto and Chicago launch plan exists; Supabase-shaped data model, migrations, realtime hooks and backend launch gate are represented.
   - Pending: production environment lock, SMS provider, server secrets, backups/monitoring, waitlist/referral tables, city density dashboard and verified invite tracking.

3. **Indian professional events**
   - Current: mixers, video speed dates, premium dinners, alumni/professional group concepts and launch-city capacity targets exist.
   - Pending: signed hosts, venue contracts, live capacity, refund policy, check-in staffing and success-story collection.

4. **Community ambassadors**
   - Current: ambassador model and referral rewards are represented.
   - Pending: ambassador invite codes, city ownership, quality rules and abuse controls.

5. **Admin / Trust Ops**
   - Current: moderation queue, reports, risk scoring, audit screens, SLA readiness scoring, reviewer workload, critical escalation, evidence audit and appeals/support handoff exist.
   - Pending: real staff accounts, support-desk integration, live reviewer shifts, production notification tooling.

6. **Legal / store ops**
   - Current: draft privacy, terms, community guidelines, store data-safety docs, subscription disclosure, account deletion path, reviewer-pack checks and legal/store readiness scoring exist.
   - Pending: final company/contact details, legal review, App Store privacy labels, age-rating declarations and published HTTPS URLs.

## Product rule

Do not scale growth until Trust Ops and legal/store ops are staffed. A serious dating app can recover from slow growth; it cannot recover from unsafe growth.
